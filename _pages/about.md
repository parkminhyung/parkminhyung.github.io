**Multi-Market Alpha Lab** is a systematic multi-factor quantitative research platform
covering KRX, JPX, and CHN equity markets.

The model architecture and factor design are publicly available on GitHub:
[**Multi-Factor-Alpha-Strategy**](https://github.com/parkminhyung/Multi-Factor-Alpha-Strategy){:target="_blank"}.
The strategy combines multiple factors — including momentum, value, and quality —
through an ensemble framework to generate systematic alpha signals.
Trading signals are generated automatically on a daily basis,
with position entries, exits, and rebalancing executed according to predefined strategy rules.

All performance metrics shown on the [**Performance**](/performance/) page
(returns, MDD, Sharpe ratio, etc.) and the daily trade logs / NAV data downloadable
from the [**Downloads**](/downloads/) page are produced by running the exact model
published in the repository above — they are **live-test results**, not backtests.

**Multi-Market Alpha Lab** 是一个面向 KRX（韩国）、JPX（日本）及 CHN（中国）股票市场的系统性多因子量化研究平台。

模型的架构与因子设计已在 GitHub 仓库公开：
[**Multi-Factor-Alpha-Strategy**](https://github.com/parkminhyung/Multi-Factor-Alpha-Strategy){:target="_blank"}。
该策略通过集成框架融合动量、价值、质量等多个因子，系统性地生成阿尔法信号。
交易信号每日自动生成，仓位的建立、平仓及再平衡均依据预设策略规则执行。

[**Performance**](/performance/) 页面所展示的业绩指标（收益率、最大回撤、夏普比率等），
以及 [**Downloads**](/downloads/) 页面提供下载的每日交易记录与净值数据，
均为上方 GitHub 仓库公开模型的**实盘运行（live-test）结果**，并非回测结果。
